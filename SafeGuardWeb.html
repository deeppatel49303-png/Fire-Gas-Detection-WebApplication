HOME

Welcome to SafeGuard — your IoT safety dashboard for real-time gas, fire and camera monitoring. This system is designed to help you stay aware of critical conditions, protect your space, and respond quickly to emergencies.

System summary

Monitor gas levels with MQ-2, detect flames with a KY-026 sensor, and view live ESP32-CAM footage. SafeGuard keeps all critical alerts in one unified dashboard.

Key features
  • Live gas concentration and danger warnings
  • Flame detection plus automatic SMS alerts
  • ESP32-CAM streaming for remote visual checks
  • Manual valve control and GPS status overview
Why SafeGuard?

Fast notifications, clear visuals, and a secure interface make it easier to act before a situation becomes dangerous. Use it for home, lab, or remote IoT security projects.

Quick tips
  1. Keep ESP32 devices powered and connected
  2. Verify IP addresses for sensor and camera devices
  3. Test emergency alerts regularly
